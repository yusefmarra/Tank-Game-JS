#### Canvas Game

## Objective
Make a basic game using canvas and no outside libraries.

## Feature List
1. Player controlled entity.
1. Movement based on the orientation of the entity.
1. Basic Enemy AI
1. Basic Level Structure (ie obstacles)
1. Score counter and display on DOM
1. OOP approach

## Stretch Goals
1. Replace rects with sprites
1. Enhance enemy AI (non-random movement, firing at Player)
1. More detailed sprite-based levels
1. Score persistence, leader board.
